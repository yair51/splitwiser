Frequently Asked Questions

To create a group, click on the "Create a New Group" button on your dashboard. You'll be prompted to enter a group name and an optional description. Once created, you can invite others to join your group.

Go to the group you want to add an expense to. Click the "Add Expense" button. You can either upload a receipt or manually enter the expense details. Make sure to select the participants who are sharing the expense.

Splitwiser uses a sophisticated algorithm to calculate balances based on the expenses and participants in each group. You can see the detailed breakdown of balances on the group details page.
